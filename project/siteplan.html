Site Purpose:

The purpose of the Community Connect Events website is to serve as a comprehensive hub for local community members, providing up-to-date information on various events and activities happening within the community. The website will offer the following services and information:


  • Event Listings: A detailed and searchable list of upcoming events, including date, time, location, and descriptions, to help community members find events that interest them.
  • Event Registration: A user-friendly registration form allowing community members to sign up for events directly through the website.
  • Contact Information: A contact page featuring a form for inquiries or event suggestions, as well as contact details for event organizers.
  • Community Overview: An introduction to the community, highlighting its features and attractions to engage new and existing members.

    • Scenario 1:

    Question: "I'm new to the community and looking to get involved. How can I find out what events are happening nearby?"
    Answer: The Home Page and Events Page of the website will provide a comprehensive list of upcoming events, complete with detailed descriptions, dates, times, and locations. Users can search for events that match their interests and filter events by category or date. The Home Page will feature highlighted events to draw attention to key activities.


    Scenario 2:

    Question: "I want to organize a community event. How can I share my event details with others and manage registrations?"
    Answer: The Contact Page will include a form for community members to submit inquiries or suggestions for new events. Event organizers can contact the site administrators to have their events listed on the Events Page. The Events Page will also feature a registration form for attendees, allowing organizers to manage and track event participation
