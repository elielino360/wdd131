Discover Delta State, Nigeria

Delta State, in southern Nigeria, is a culturally rich and economically vital region. It borders Edo, Anambra, Rivers, and Bayelsa States, as well as the Atlantic Ocean. The state is home to diverse ethnic groups, including the Urhobo, Itsekiri, Isoko, Ijaw, and Anioma (Igbo).
